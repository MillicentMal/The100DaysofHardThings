About 100 Days of Hard Things

The 100 Days of Hard Things is a project to help a struggling procrastinator get out of the Dark Playground. The aim is to do a project - deploy something, anything. Everyday after learning to develop her skills and also get better at fighting off procrastination. This will be considered the landing page with 100 mini projects deployed by the end of the 100 days, and 3 major projects. All projects will come from Frontend Mentors Website and maybe some from the The Odin Project and some definitely from freecodecamp.

Projects

Herein lies projects from the 100 days of Hard things. These will be listed in card format and will contain a mini preview, and when one clicks there will be a link to the actual mini project, project description, and code. A short writeup of the concepts, and what was learnt through each project will be included in the README.md
